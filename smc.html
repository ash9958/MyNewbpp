Student Mentoring Cell


Function of the Cell

  1. Inculcate the students discipline, punctuality and regularity

  2. Enable the parents to know about the performance and regularity of their wards

  3. Address conflicts in attitudes, habits and knowledge of students towards learning practices

  4. Establish teacher-student relationship

  5. Counselling student for solving their problem and provide confidence for their personal growth

  6. Guiding students to choose right career path for job, higher studies, entrepreneurship etc.
